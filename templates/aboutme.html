About Me

Hi! My name is Iris Kuhn. My former self was a hospital pharmacist. I always like to find different ways to help improve efficiency and I believe software engineering can help us achieve that, and more!

In August last year, timing was right, and I decided to dive in and pursue software engineering at Hackbright. I hope with what I learned from Hackbright and self-studying after graduation, I will be better equipped to build software that helps improve productivity, allowing us to focus on solving other issues or problems.

Why I Created This App

When I was planning my wedding, I was having a hard time balancing my checking account because all the one-time big expenses, like floral arrangement and venue, were dued at different days. I didn't want to run into negative balance and pay extra fees so I started tracking them in a spreadsheet.

I found it was super helpful for me to see my projected budget and I have been using this spreadsheet ever since. I hope you will find this app helpful as well!
